Social Assistance Outsourcing

Joint project with Tim Layton and Derek Wu examining state choices around modernizing eligibility systems for SNAP, TANF, and Medicaid — specifically whether to modernize, when to modernize, and whether to build in-house or outsource to vendors (e.g., Deloitte, Accenture). We compile 50-state modernization histories and link them to SNAP administrative outcomes (administrative costs, error rates, access) and contextual indicators (ACS rent, QCEW wages, LAUS unemployment). The Stata pipeline cleans/merges sources into an analysis-ready panel and produces descriptive figures, fixed-effects/event-study analyses, and robustness checks.
